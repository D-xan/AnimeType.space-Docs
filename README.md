# AnimeType Official Documentation Portal

Welcome to the official documentation repository for **AnimeType** — the premier social networking destination exclusively designed for anime, manga, and light novel enthusiasts across the globe.

## Our Vision & Mission
We built AnimeType because we believe the anime community deserves a dedicated space free from the noise of generic social media. Our mission is to provide a platform where fans can dive deep into lore, share artistic creations, and connect with fellow enthusiasts in real-time.

## Key Features
- **Deep Community Engagement:** Join discussions, share fan theories, and rate anime & manga.
- **Identity & Aura System:** Earn Aura points and climb the global leaderboards based on your community contributions.
- **Anime & Manga Tracking:** Manage your watch and read lists, rate your favorites, and discover new series.
- **Real-time Chat:** Encrypted, secure, and instant direct messaging with fellow fans.
- **Creator Dashboard:** Detailed analytics for content creators to track engagement and growth.
