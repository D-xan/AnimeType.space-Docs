## What is AnimeType?

**AnimeType** is a dedicated social platform for the global anime community. Unlike general-purpose social networks, every feature is designed around anime and manga culture: fans can publish posts and polls, write and vote on **fan theories**, track the shows they are watching in a personal **Anime Journey**, earn reputation through an **Identity Score (“Aura”)**, collect achievement **badges**, climb a global **leaderboard**, and chat in real time with other fans.

AnimeType is **free to use**. There is currently no paid subscription or in-app purchase system.

## Key features at a glance

- **🏠 Community feed** — posts, images, and interactive polls from fans you follow and the wider community.
- **🧠 Fan theories** — publish predictions and analysis about your favorite series and let the community vote.
- **⭐ Aura (Identity Score)** — a reputation system that rewards genuine, high-quality contributions.
- **🏅 Badges & ranks** — collect achievement badges and climb tiered identity ranks.
- **📈 Global leaderboard** — see how your Aura compares with fans worldwide.
- **📖 Anime Journey** — track what you're watching, completed, or plan to watch across anime and manga.
- **💬 Real-time chat** — direct and group messaging, including voice and video calls.
- **📊 Creator analytics** — understand how your posts and theories perform.
- **🔎 Discovery & search** — find creators, series, and trending discussions.
