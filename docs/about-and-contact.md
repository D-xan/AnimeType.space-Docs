# About AnimeType & Contact

## Our mission

**AnimeType exists to give anime fans a home of their own** — a vibrant, safe, and engaging space where enthusiasts can connect, share theories, discover new series, and express their otaku identity. We believe anime is more than entertainment; it's a global culture that brings people together.

Every feature is built around that belief: from fan theories and the Aura reputation system to anime tracking and creator analytics, AnimeType is designed to help fans do more than scroll — to contribute, be recognized, and belong.

## Core values

1. **Passion first** — we celebrate the passion of the anime community.
2. **Safe haven** — we strictly enforce guidelines to keep the platform welcoming and free of toxicity.
3. **Creator support** — we highlight and reward creators, reviewers, artists, and theorists.

## The founder

AnimeType was founded by **Dhruba Singha Roy**, who built the platform out of a passion for anime and technology. With a background in software engineering, web technologies, and scalable application architecture, his focus is on building products that prioritize performance, simplicity, and long-term sustainability.

His vision is to make AnimeType one of the world's leading communities for anime enthusiasts — a place where every fan, creator, reviewer, and theorist can build a lasting reputation and share their passion.
