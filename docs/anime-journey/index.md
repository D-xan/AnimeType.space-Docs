# Your Anime Journey

AnimeType is not just a social network; it's also a powerful tool for tracking your personal anime and manga journey.

## Tracking Progress
Navigate to your Profile to access your **Media Library**.
- **Watch/Read Status:** Mark titles as *Completed*, *Watching/Reading*, *Paused*, *Dropped*, or *Plan to Watch*.
- **Favorites:** Pin your absolute favorite series to the top of your profile to show off your taste.

## Community Integration
When you review an anime or update your progress, you can choose to share it directly to the Home Feed. This allows your friends to see what you are currently enjoying and sparks organic conversations.

## Achievements & Goals
Set personal goals (e.g., "Watch 50 anime this year") and unlock community achievements as you reach major milestones in your tracking journey.
