# Anime & Manga Journey Tracking

AnimeType includes a built-in **Media Dashboard** on your profile, allowing you to showcase what you are currently watching or reading.

## Tracked Statistics
The dashboard aggregates your lifetime otaku stats:
- Total **Series** and **Movies** watched
- Total **Anime Episodes** consumed
- Total **Manga** and **Manhwa** series read
- Total **Chapters** read

## Managing Your List
You can add media to your list via the search bar. Each entry allows you to define:
- **Status**: *Watching/Reading, Completed, Plan to Watch, On-Hold, Dropped*
- **Progress**: Episodes/Chapters completed
- **Score**: Your personal rating
- **Visibility**: *Public* (visible on your profile) or *Private* (only visible to you)

Completing media automatically grants you Identity points!
