# Anime & Manga Tracking (Anime Journey)

**Your Anime Journey is a personal library on [AnimeType](https://www.animetype.space) for tracking everything you watch and read.** It keeps your progress in one place and showcases your taste on your profile.

## What is the Anime Journey?

The Anime Journey is AnimeType's built-in tracker for anime, manga, and manhwa. Add titles you're interested in, update your status as you go, and build a living record of your fandom.

## What you can track

- **Status** — mark each title as *watching*, *completed*, or *plan to watch*.
- **Progress** — record how many episodes or chapters you've finished.
- **Ratings** — rate the series you've experienced.
- **Favorites** — highlight the titles that mean the most to you.

## Why track your anime on AnimeType?

- **Everything in one place** — your watchlist lives right alongside the community, so you can discuss what you're watching as you go.
- **Show your taste** — your Journey appears on your profile, helping other fans discover shared interests.
- **Better discovery** — what you track helps surface relevant discussions and recommendations.
- **Fuel your identity** — an active, curated Journey is part of building your presence in the community.

## How to add a title

1. Open your profile or the Anime Journey section.
2. Search for the anime or manga you want to add.
3. Choose a status (watching, completed, or plan to watch) and save.
4. Update your progress and ratings any time.

Series information is sourced from public anime databases so you can quickly find and add titles.
