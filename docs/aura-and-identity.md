# Aura & the Identity System

**Aura is AnimeType's reputation system — an Identity Score that reflects how much you contribute to the community.** It is one of the platform's defining features, designed to reward genuine, high-quality participation rather than follower counts alone.

## What is Aura?

Aura (also called your **Identity Score**) is a number that grows as you take meaningful actions on [AnimeType](https://www.animetype.space): publishing posts, joining discussions, creating polls, and writing fan theories that the community appreciates. The more you contribute constructively, the higher your Aura climbs.

## Why does Aura matter?

- **Recognition** — Aura signals that you're a trusted, active member of the community.
- **Identity ranks** — as your Aura grows, you progress through **tiered ranks** that represent your standing.
- **Leaderboard** — your Aura determines your position on AnimeType's **global leaderboard**.
- **Badges** — reaching milestones unlocks achievement **badges** displayed on your profile.

## Identity ranks

AnimeType organizes reputation into escalating rank tiers. As you earn Aura, you advance from newcomer tiers toward the most prestigious ranks reserved for the community's most dedicated fans. Each tier is represented visually with distinct badge medallions on your profile.

## Badges

Badges are collectible achievements that mark milestones — such as joining the community, hitting contribution streaks, reaching global ranks, and other accomplishments. They range from common starter badges to rare, high-tier honors, giving every member goals to work toward.

## Creator analytics

Contributors can view a personal **analytics dashboard** that shows how their content performs — views, engagement, reach, and audience trends. This helps creators understand what resonates and grow their influence within the community.

## How to grow your Aura

1. **Post thoughtfully** — share genuine, interesting content about the series you love.
2. **Write fan theories** — well-argued, highly-voted theories are among the best ways to build reputation. See [Fan Theories](fan-theories.md).
3. **Engage** — comment, discuss, and participate in polls.
4. **Be consistent** — steady, quality contributions compound over time.

> Aura rewards quality and authenticity. Spam, abuse, and manipulation work against you and are addressed through moderation — see [Safety & Privacy](safety-and-privacy.md).
