# Aura & Identity System

Your presence on AnimeType is quantified by your **Identity Score**. As you participate in the community, your score increases, unlocking new **Ranks** and visual **Auras**.

## How to Earn Points
Your Identity Score grows dynamically based on the impact you have on the platform. Earning points is tied to content creation and engagement. While the exact backend algorithms include spam prevention and decay modifiers, the core point system rewards:
- Receiving Likes on your posts
- Receiving Comments and generating discussions
- Having your posts Shared
- Attracting visits to your Profile
- Creating high-quality Posts and Fan Theories
- Updating your Anime/Manga Journey (e.g., Completing a series)

## Ranks
Your Rank is a prestigious title displayed on your profile. The ranks progress as follows:
1. **Rookie**
2. **Academy Student**
3. **Genin**
4. **Chunin**
5. **Jonin**
6. **Kage**
7. **Legendary Kage**
8. **Grand Sage**
9. **Anime Myth**
10. **Anime Legend**

## Auras
Auras are visual enhancements (colored borders, glows, and name stylings) that reflect your current standing. They act as a visual shorthand for your reputation.
- **Grey Aura**
- **Blue Aura**
- **Emerald Aura**
- **Purple Aura**
- **Golden Aura**
- **Crimson Aura**
- **Prism Aura** (A dynamic blend of Purple, Pink, and Gold)
- **Eclipse Aura** (A legendary dark aesthetic)

*Keep engaging, posting quality theories, and updating your watch list to reach the legendary Eclipse Aura!*
