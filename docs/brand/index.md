# Brand Guidelines

AnimeType's brand identity reflects the vibrant and diverse world of anime.

## Colors
Our signature colors are Deep Purple and Electric Blue, often used with glassmorphism effects.

## Typography
We use **Inter** for UI elements and body copy, and **Outfit** for headings and stylistic text.
