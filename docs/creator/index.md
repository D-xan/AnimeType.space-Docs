# Creator Center

Welcome to the Creator Center! Here you'll learn how to build your audience, create engaging content, and understand your analytics.

## Creating Quality Posts
- **Visuals Matter:** Use high-quality images for your posts and reviews.
- **Engaging Captions:** Ask questions to encourage comments.
- **Use the Double Hashtag Feature:** Tag both the anime series and the specific topic (e.g., `#OnePiece #Theory`).

## Polls & Fan Theories
- **10-Option Polls:** Use polls to settle anime debates. These historically receive the highest engagement rates.
- **Fan Theories:** Write detailed, well-thought-out theories. Theories use a unique tri-state voting system (Agree, Disagree, Interesting) that heavily boosts your Identity Score.

## Creator Dashboard Analytics
Track your performance using the built-in analytics dashboard on your profile. Monitor your:
- Profile Visits
- Engagement Rate (Likes, Comments, Shares, Saves)
- Follower Growth
- Unique Viewers
