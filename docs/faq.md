# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Common questions about **[AnimeType](https://www.animetype.space)**, the free social network for anime and manga fans.

## What is AnimeType?

AnimeType is a free social networking platform built specifically for anime, manga, and manhwa fans. You can share posts and polls, write and vote on fan theories, track your watchlist, earn reputation, and chat with other fans. It is available on the web at [animetype.space](https://www.animetype.space) and as an Android app.

## Is AnimeType free?

Yes. AnimeType is completely free to use. There is currently no subscription, premium plan, or in-app purchase.

## How do I sign up for AnimeType?

Visit [www.animetype.space](https://www.animetype.space) or open the Android app, create an account with your email (or a supported sign-in option), confirm you meet the 18+ age requirement, and set up your profile. You can start posting, following others, and tracking anime right away.

## What is the minimum age to use AnimeType?

AnimeType is strictly for users **18 years of age or older**. Accounts found to belong to minors are removed. See our [Child Safety Policy](https://www.animetype.space/legal/child-safety).

## What is "Aura" on AnimeType?

Aura is AnimeType's Identity Score — a reputation system that increases when you make genuine, high-quality contributions such as posting, participating in discussions, and publishing well-received fan theories. Higher Aura unlocks identity ranks and a spot on the global leaderboard. Read the [Aura & Identity guide](aura-and-identity.md).

## What are fan theories?

Fan theories are posts where you share predictions or analysis about a series. Other users vote on them, so the most compelling theories rise to the top. Learn more in the [Fan Theories guide](fan-theories.md).

## Can I track the anime and manga I watch?

Yes. Your **Anime Journey** lets you mark titles as watching, completed, or plan-to-watch, track episode progress, and rate series. See [Anime & Manga Tracking](anime-tracking.md).

## Does AnimeType have chat?

Yes. AnimeType supports private direct messages, group chats, and voice and video calls.

## Is AnimeType available as a mobile app?

AnimeType is available as an **Android app**, and the website works as an installable progressive web app on both mobile and desktop.

## How is my data handled?

AnimeType collects only what's needed to run the service and is transparent about analytics and advertising. You can access, export, correct, or delete your data. Read our [Privacy Policy](https://www.animetype.space/legal/privacy) and [Cookie Policy](https://www.animetype.space/legal/cookies).

## How do I delete my account?

Go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**. Your account enters a 30-day grace period (during which you can restore it by logging back in), after which your personal data is permanently deleted. See the [Data Retention & Deletion Policy](https://www.animetype.space/legal/deletion).

## How do I report a user, post, or safety concern?

Use the in-app report option (the "…" menu on any post or profile). For urgent child-safety concerns, email **support@animetype.space**. See [Safety & Privacy](safety-and-privacy.md).

## Is AnimeType affiliated with any anime studio?

No. AnimeType is an independent platform and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any anime studio, publisher, or rights holder.

- General support: **support@animetype.space**
- Business, press, and official inquiries: **official@animetype.space**
