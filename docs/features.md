# AnimeType Features

A complete overview of what you can do on **[AnimeType](https://www.animetype.space)** — the free social network for anime and manga fans.

## Community feed

The home feed is your window into the community. It blends posts from people you follow with trending and recommended content from across AnimeType.

- **Rich posts** — share text, images, and links about any series.
- **Polls** — create interactive polls with up to ten options and watch results update live.
- **Likes, comments, and saves** — engage with posts, hold threaded discussions, and bookmark content for later.
- **Reposts** — amplify posts you love to your own followers.

## Fan theories

Fan theories are a signature part of AnimeType. Publish your predictions and analysis about ongoing or completed series, and let the community **vote** on how convincing they are. Well-argued theories earn recognition and boost your Aura. Learn more in the [Fan Theories guide](fan-theories.md).

## Anime & manga tracking (Anime Journey)

Keep a personal library of everything you watch and read. Mark titles as *watching*, *completed*, or *plan to watch*, record episode progress, rate series, and showcase your taste on your profile. See the [Anime Tracking guide](anime-tracking.md).

## Aura — the Identity Score

**Aura** is AnimeType's reputation system. It grows as you make genuine, high-quality contributions — posting, discussing, and publishing theories the community values. Higher Aura unlocks tiered **identity ranks** and reflects your standing in the community. Full details in [Aura & Identity](aura-and-identity.md).

## Badges & achievements

Earn collectible **badges** that mark milestones and accomplishments across your journey — from your first steps on the platform to rare, high-tier achievements. Badges are displayed on your profile.

## Global leaderboard

See how your Aura ranks against fans worldwide on the global **leaderboard**, updated regularly. It's a friendly way to celebrate the community's most active and respected members.

## Real-time chat

Connect directly with other fans through:

- **Direct messages** — private one-to-one conversations.
- **Group chats** — talk with multiple fans at once.
- **Voice & video calls** — jump into real-time calls with friends.

## Profiles

Your profile is your identity hub: avatar, bio, anime interests, posts, badges, Aura, and your Anime Journey — all in one place. Privacy controls let you decide what's visible.

## Creator analytics

Active contributors get a personal **analytics dashboard** showing how their posts and theories perform — views, engagement, reach, and audience insights — so creators can understand and grow their impact.

## Discovery & search

Find creators, series, hashtags, and trending discussions through fast search and curated discovery, so there's always something new to explore.

## Notifications

Stay in the loop with real-time notifications for likes, comments, follows, mentions, replies, new badges, and rank changes.

## Platforms & availability

- **Web** — a fast progressive web app at [www.animetype.space](https://www.animetype.space), installable to your home screen.
- **Android** — the AnimeType Android app.

AnimeType is **free**, with no payment system at this time.
