# Getting Started

Welcome to AnimeType! This guide will help you become productive immediately.

## Creating an Account
1. Download the app from the Play Store or visit [animetype.space](https://www.animetype.space).
2. Choose **Sign Up** and enter your email address and a secure password.
3. Verify your email address through the link sent to your inbox.

## Setting Up Your Profile
- **Avatar & Bio:** Navigate to the Profile tab. Tap **Edit Profile** to upload an avatar and write a bio that reflects your anime interests.
- **Anime Profile:** Track your favorite anime and manga to help the recommendation engine find like-minded friends.

## Creating Your First Post
Tap the **(+)** button in the center of the bottom navigation bar. You can add images, write a review, create 10-option polls, or post a fan theory. 

![Create Post](/assets/screenshots/feed.png)

## Following Users & Discovering Content
- Use the **Search** tab to find friends or trending anime.
- Engage with posts on your **Home Feed** by liking, commenting, or bookmarking.
- The more you interact, the better your feed recommendations will become!
