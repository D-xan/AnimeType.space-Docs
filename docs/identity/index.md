# Identity System & Aura

AnimeType rewards valuable community contributions through the **Identity System**.

## What is Aura?
Aura is a measure of your positive influence within the community. You earn Aura when your content receives engagement.

- **Creating Quality Posts:** High-quality theories, reviews, and polls naturally generate more engagement.
- **Community Interaction:** Helping others, receiving likes on your comments, and getting shares.

## Creator Titles & Ranks
As your Aura grows, you unlock new Creator Titles. These titles are displayed on your profile and next to your name in discussions.
- Genin -> Chunin -> Jonin -> Kage -> Legendary Kage -> Grand Sage -> Anime Myth -> Anime Legend

## Badges & Verification
- **Badges:** Earned through special community milestones (e.g., Early Adopter, Top Theorist).
- **Verification:** Creators who consistently maintain high engagement and positive community standing may apply for the verified checkmark.

## Leaderboards
The global leaderboard ranks the top creators based on their Aura and recent Weekly Growth. Climbing the leaderboard increases your visibility across the platform.
