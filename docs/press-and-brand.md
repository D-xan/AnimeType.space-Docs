# Press & Brand

Resources for journalists, bloggers, content creators, and anyone writing about or linking to **AnimeType**. You're welcome to reference the facts below and link to [animetype.space](https://www.animetype.space).

## One-line description

> AnimeType is a free social network and community platform built specifically for anime, manga, and manhwa fans.

## Short description (≈50 words)

> AnimeType ([animetype.space](https://www.animetype.space)) is a free, anime-first social network where fans share posts and polls, write and vote on fan theories, track their watchlist, and build reputation through the "Aura" identity system. It's an 18+ community available on the web and Android.

## Boilerplate (≈100 words)

> AnimeType is a social networking platform made specifically for the global anime, manga, and manhwa community. Founded by Dhruba Singha Roy, it combines a community feed, fan theories with community voting, anime and manga tracking, real-time chat, and a reputation system called "Aura" that rewards genuine contributions with ranks, badges, and a global leaderboard. AnimeType is free to use, strictly for users aged 18 and over, and available on the web and as an Android app. It is an independent platform, not affiliated with any anime studio or publisher. Learn more at animetype.space.
