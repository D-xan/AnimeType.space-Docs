# Privacy Center

At AnimeType, we believe your data belongs to you. This center explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Data Collection & Retention
We only collect data necessary to provide and improve our services (e.g., your anime preferences, posts, and interactions). We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## Your Privacy Controls
You can adjust your privacy settings via **Settings > Privacy**:
- **Private Profile:** Hide your posts and tracking lists from non-followers.
- **Leaderboard Visibility:** Opt-out of appearing on global leaderboards.
- **Follower Visibility:** Hide your followers/following lists.

## Account Deletion & Data Export
- **Delete Account:** You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from the Settings menu.
- **Data Export:** Request a copy of your data (posts, comments, tracking lists) by contacting support.
