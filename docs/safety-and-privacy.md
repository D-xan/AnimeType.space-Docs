# Safety & Privacy

The safety of our community and the privacy of your data are core priorities at **[AnimeType](https://www.animetype.space)**. This page summarizes our approach and links to the full policies.

## An 18+ community

AnimeType is strictly for users **18 years of age or older**. This keeps the platform focused on mature discussion and simplifies moderation. Accounts found to belong to minors are removed. Full details: [Child Safety Policy](https://www.animetype.space/legal/child-safety).

## Zero tolerance for exploitation

We maintain a **zero-tolerance policy** toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, and any content that exploits or endangers minors — in any form, real or fictional. Violations result in immediate removal, a permanent ban, and reporting to the appropriate authorities, including organizations such as NCMEC and relevant law-enforcement agencies.

## Moderation & reporting

- **Report anything** — every post and profile has a report option in its "…" menu.
- **Community Guidelines** — clear rules prohibit harassment, hate speech, doxxing, and illegal content. Read the [Community Guidelines](https://www.animetype.space/legal/guidelines).
- **Trust & Safety** — reports are reviewed, and repeat offenders lose access.
- **Urgent concerns** — email **support@animetype.space** for critical safety issues.

## Your privacy & data

We are transparent about what we collect and why:

- **You control your data** — access, export, correct, or delete it from your settings.
- **Account deletion** — a 30-day grace period lets you change your mind before permanent deletion. See the [Data Retention & Deletion Policy](https://www.animetype.space/legal/deletion).
- **Cookies, analytics & ads** — we use analytics and advertising to keep AnimeType free, and provide consent controls (including a "Do Not Sell or Share" option where applicable). See the [Cookie Policy](https://www.animetype.space/legal/cookies).
- **Your rights** — depending on your region, you have rights under laws such as the GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and India's DPDP Act. Read the [Privacy Policy](https://www.animetype.space/legal/privacy).
