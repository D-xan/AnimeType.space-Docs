# Support Center

Need help? You've come to the right place.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How do I change my username?**
A: Go to Profile > Edit Profile. You can change your username once every 30 days.

**Q: My Aura score didn't update after I got likes.**
A: Aura scores are recalculated periodically. Please allow a short time for the system to process recent engagements.

**Q: How do I delete a post or comment?**
A: Tap the three dots (⋮) on your post or comment and select 'Delete'.

## Contact Us
If you encountered a bug or need account assistance:
- **Email:** support@animetype.space
- **Response Time:** Please expect a response within 1 to 3 business days.

When reporting bugs, please include your device model, OS version, and steps to reproduce the issue.
