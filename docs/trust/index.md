# Trust & Safety Center

Your safety is our top priority. We are committed to maintaining a toxic-free, welcoming, and inclusive environment.

## Managing Interactions
- **Blocking:** Prevents a user from seeing your profile, posts, or contacting you.
- **Muting:** Removes a user's posts from your feed without them knowing.
- **Reporting:** Flag content or accounts that violate our Community Guidelines.

## What We Do Not Tolerate
- Harassment and Bullying
- Hate Speech
- Spam and Scams
- NSFW and Illegal Content
- Impersonation

## How to Report
You can report a post, comment, or user profile by clicking the **three dots (⋮)** menu on the content and selecting **Report**. Our moderation team reviews reports within 24-48 hours.

## Appeals
If your account was actioned by mistake, you may submit an appeal through the Support Center.
