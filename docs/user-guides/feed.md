# Home Feed

The Home Feed is the center of the AnimeType experience, providing a personalized stream of content based on your interests and whom you follow.

## How It Works
The feed intelligently aggregates:
- **Posts from friends:** People you follow.
- **Trending content:** Highly engaged posts, reviews, and polls from the global community.
- **Recommended theories:** Fan theories matching your anime watchlist.

## User Interface
- **Post Cards:** Rich media cards containing images, text, or interactive polls.
- **Action Bar:** Quick access to Like, Comment, Share, and Bookmark.
- **Navigation:** Easily switch between 'Following' and 'Global' feeds.

![Home Feed](/assets/screenshots/feed.png)

## Best Practices
- **Engage:** Liking and commenting helps tailor your future recommendations.
- **Refresh:** Pull to refresh to instantly get the newest posts.
- **Use Filters:** Utilize hashtag filters to focus on specific anime discussions.
