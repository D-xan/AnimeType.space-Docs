# 💬 Direct Messaging & Chat Settings

## Connecting Beyond the Feed

While the AnimeType feed is perfect for public debates and sharing content with the world, some conversations are better suited for private spaces. Whether you want to discuss a massive spoiler with a friend, collaborate on a joint fan theory, or just say hello to a creator you admire, AnimeType's Direct Messaging (DM) system provides a blazing-fast, real-time chat experience.

## ✉️ Starting a New Conversation

Initiating a chat is incredibly simple. 
1. Navigate to the profile of the user you wish to message.
2. Click the "Message" or "Chat" icon located near their follow button.
3. Alternatively, you can open your Inbox (the mail icon on your navigation bar), click the "New Message" button, and search for a username to start a thread.

### Sharing Posts in Chat
If you see an amazing post or theory on your feed that you want to discuss privately, you don't need to copy and paste the link! Simply click the "Share" icon beneath the post and select "Send in Direct Message." You can select the friend you want to send it to, and the post will be beautifully embedded directly into your chat thread.

## ⚙️ Chat Privacy Settings (Message Requests)

To protect our users from spam and unwanted messages, AnimeType utilizes a robust Message Request system. You have full control over who is allowed to slide into your DMs.

By navigating to **Settings > Privacy > Chat Settings**, you can choose from three inbox modes:

1. **Open Inbox**: Anyone on AnimeType can send you a direct message, and it will immediately appear in your primary inbox and trigger a push notification.
2. **Message Requests (Recommended)**: If a user who *you do not follow* attempts to message you, their message will be routed to a hidden "Message Requests" folder. You will not receive a push notification. You can review the message safely—the sender will not know you have read it—and choose to "Accept" (moving it to your main inbox) or "Delete/Block."
3. **Closed Inbox**: Only users who you actively follow can send you messages. Anyone else attempting to message you will receive an error stating your inbox is closed.

## 👁️ Read Receipts and Typing Indicators

AnimeType's chat is built on real-time WebSockets, meaning it feels incredibly alive and responsive.
- **Typing Indicators**: When the person you are chatting with starts typing, you will see a small animated bubble indicating that a message is incoming.
- **Read Receipts**: When you send a message, it will display a single checkmark (Sent), then a double checkmark (Delivered). Once the recipient opens the chat window and actually views the message, the checkmarks will turn blue (Read).

*Note: If you value your privacy and prefer not to let others know when you have read their messages, you can disable Read Receipts globally in your Chat Settings. However, doing so will also prevent you from seeing when others have read your messages.*

## 🗑️ Managing Your Inbox

Over time, your inbox may become cluttered. You have several options for managing your chat history:
- **Deleting Messages**: You can long-press (or right-click) any individual message you sent and select "Delete." This will remove the message for both you and the recipient.
- **Deleting a Conversation**: You can swipe left on any chat thread in your inbox to delete the entire conversation. Note that this only deletes the history on *your* device; the other user will still have their copy of the chat log.
- **Reporting**: If someone sends you inappropriate content, harassment, or spam, please long-press the message and select "Report." Our moderation team takes chat reports very seriously and will take swift action to protect the community.\n
