# Creating Posts

AnimeType offers rich tools for sharing your thoughts, memes, and updates.

## Standard Posts
A standard post is perfect for quick thoughts, questions, or image sharing. Features include:
- **Media Uploads**: Attach images or videos to your post.
- **Polls**: Add a multi-option poll to gather community opinions.
- **Character Tagging**: Tag specific anime characters in your post so fans tracking that character can find it.
- **User Tagging**: Mention specific users.

## Advanced Publishing Options
- **Collaborators**: Invite another user as a Co-Author. The post will appear on both of your profiles!
- **Post Anonymously**: Have a spicy hot take? You can toggle the "Anonymous" option to hide your identity. Note that anonymous posts still adhere to Community Guidelines and moderation.

To get started, click the massive **"+"** button on your home feed.
