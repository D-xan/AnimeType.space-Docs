# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the AnimeType FAQ! Before reaching out to our support team, please scan through this list of our most commonly asked user questions. Chances are, the solution to your issue is detailed right here.

---

### 👤 Account & Profile Management

**Q: Can I change my @username?**
A: Yes! You can change your unique `@username` once every 30 days. Navigate to Settings > Account > Edit Username. Please note that once you change your username, your old one becomes instantly available for anyone else to claim. Your display name (the bold text above your username) can be changed as often as you like.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: If you wish to permanently leave AnimeType, go to Settings > Account > Danger Zone and select "Delete Account." This action is irreversible. All of your posts, theories, comments, and chat logs will be permanently erased from our servers in compliance with data privacy regulations.

**Q: Why are my profile analytics not updating instantly?**
A: To keep AnimeType lightning-fast, we cache profile analytics for approximately 1 hour. If you just received a massive influx of views or likes, please wait an hour for the backend servers to recalculate your lifetime statistics and update your dashboard.

---

### 📝 Posting & Content

**Q: What is the maximum image size I can upload?**
A: You can upload images up to 8MB in size. We support JPG, PNG, WEBP, and GIF formats. Our servers will automatically compress and optimize the images for mobile viewing while retaining high quality.

**Q: Why was my post removed?**
A: Posts are typically removed by our moderation team if they violate our Community Guidelines. The most common reasons include:
1. Posting untagged, massive spoilers for currently airing anime or unreleased manga chapters.
2. Harassment or toxic behavior in the comments.
3. Posting Not-Safe-For-Work (NSFW) or explicitly adult content, which is strictly prohibited on AnimeType.

**Q: Can I edit a post after I publish it?**
A: Yes! You can edit the text of a post for up to 24 hours after publishing. However, you cannot change the attached images or alter the core category (e.g., changing a "Post" into a "Theory") once it is live.

### 🔒 Privacy & Interactions

**Q: Someone is harassing me in DMs. What should I do?**
A: Please do not engage with the user. Long-press on their message and select "Report." Then, navigate to their profile, click the three-dot options menu, and select "Block." They will be instantly permanently removed from your AnimeType experience, and our moderation team will review the report for potential account suspension.

**Q: How do I switch between Light Mode and Dark Mode?**
A: AnimeType respects your device's system settings by default. If your phone is in Dark Mode, the app will render in Dark Mode. If you want to force a specific theme, go to Settings > Display & Appearance and manually toggle your preferred theme.

**Q: Why is my Agreement Percentage Ring not changing when people vote "Interesting" on my theory?**
A: This is by design! The "Interesting" vote is a neutral metric that rewards creativity but does not indicate factual consensus. The percentage ring is calculated exclusively by comparing "Agree" votes against "Disagree" votes. For a full breakdown, please read our [Theories & Voting Guide](./theories-and-voting.md).
