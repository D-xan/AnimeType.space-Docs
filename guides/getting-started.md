# 🚀 Getting Started on AnimeType

Welcome to AnimeType! If you've just created your account, you might be wondering where to begin. This comprehensive guide will walk you through setting up your profile, navigating the interface, finding content you love, and making your very first post. Let's dive in!

## 🎨 Customizing Your Profile

Your profile is your digital identity within the AnimeType community. Taking a few minutes to personalize it will drastically improve your experience and help you connect with like-minded fans.

1. **Upload an Avatar**: Navigate to your profile page and click the "Edit Profile" button. Choose a high-quality profile picture. Whether it's your favorite anime character, an original illustration, or a photo of yourself, this is how the community will recognize you in the feed and in chats.
2. **Set a Banner Image**: Just like your avatar, your banner adds flair to your profile header. We recommend using a landscape image (like a beautiful scenic shot from a Makoto Shinkai film) for the best fit.
3. **Write a Bio**: Use the bio section to introduce yourself. List your current top 3 favorite anime, the manga you are currently reading, or your favorite genres. A good bio is the easiest way to attract followers who share your specific tastes!

## 🗺️ Navigating the Home Feed

The AnimeType Home Feed is where you will spend most of your time. It is divided into two distinct tabs to give you full control over what you see:

- **The 'For You' Feed**: This is our algorithmic discovery feed. It showcases trending posts, popular fan theories, and highly engaged content from across the entire AnimeType platform. This is the best place to discover new creators, find viral anime memes, and read thought-provoking analyses from people you don't follow yet.
- **The 'Following' Feed**: This feed is strictly chronological and only displays content from the users you have explicitly chosen to follow. It ensures that you never miss an update, artwork, or review from your friends and favorite creators.

## 🔍 Using the Search & Discovery Page

Looking for something specific? The Search Page (accessible via the magnifying glass icon) is your best friend.

- **Searching for Users**: Type in a username or a display name to find specific people. 
- **Searching by Tags**: AnimeType relies heavily on hashtags for organization. Try searching for `#OnePiece`, `#JujutsuKaisen`, or `#FanArt` to instantly filter the platform's content down to that specific topic.
- **Trending Topics**: The search page also highlights current trending hashtags, allowing you to see what the community is talking about right now—perfect for finding discussions immediately after a new seasonal episode drops.

## 💬 Interacting with the Community

AnimeType provides several ways to interact with content. Below every standard post, you will find an interaction bar:

- **Like (Heart)**: Show your appreciation for a post, artwork, or meme.
- **Comment**: Join the conversation! Click the comment icon to leave your thoughts, reply to other users, or start a debate.
- **Share**: Use this to send a post directly to a friend via AnimeType Direct Messages, or copy the link to share it on other social networks.
- **Save (Bookmark)**: See an amazing piece of fan art or a long theory you want to read later? Click the bookmark icon to save it privately to your profile's "Saved" tab.

*(Note: Theory posts have a slightly different interaction bar. Please refer to our [Theories & Voting Guide](./theories-and-voting.md) for more details).*

## 🌟 Making Your First Post

Ready to contribute? Click the large "+" or "Create" button on your mobile app or desktop navigation bar.

You can post text, images, or links. We highly recommend adding relevant hashtags to your post (e.g., `#AnimeReview`, `#Naruto`) so that other users can discover your content through the search page. Don't be shy—the AnimeType community is incredibly welcoming. Say hello, post a picture of your current manga collection, or share your thoughts on the anime you watched last night!\n
