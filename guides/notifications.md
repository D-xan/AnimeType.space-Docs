# 🔔 Managing Notifications & Alerts

## Staying Connected Without the Noise

In a highly active social network like AnimeType, it is easy to become overwhelmed by a constant barrage of likes, comments, and messages. We understand that notification fatigue is real, which is why we have engineered a highly granular notification management system. 

You have total control over what alerts you receive, ensuring that you stay connected to the conversations that matter most to you, without your phone buzzing unnecessarily.

## 📱 Push Notifications vs. In-App Alerts

AnimeType utilizes two distinct notification layers:

1. **In-App Notification Center**: Accessible via the "Bell" icon on your navigation bar. This is a chronological log of all interactions involving your account. It is safely contained within the app and does not alert your physical device.
2. **Push Notifications**: These are the native alerts that buzz your phone, wake up your lock screen, or drop down from the top of your desktop browser. 

By default, the AnimeType mobile app will ask for permission to send you Push Notifications when you first install it. If you accept, you will receive real-time alerts for Direct Messages and major account milestones.

## ⚙️ Customizing Your Push Preferences

To take control of your alerts, navigate to **Settings > Notifications**. Here, you can toggle specific triggers on or off for both Push Notifications and In-App Alerts.

We highly recommend customizing these to fit your lifestyle:
- **Direct Messages**: (Recommended ON). Ensure you never miss a chat from a friend.
- **Mentions & Tags**: (Recommended ON). If someone explicitly tags you in a comment to ask your opinion on a theory, you probably want to know about it.
- **New Followers**: (Optional). Fun when you are starting out, but you may want to disable this via Push once your account begins growing rapidly.
- **Likes & Saves**: (Recommended OFF for Push, ON for In-App). Getting a push notification for every single like on a viral post will drain your phone's battery. We recommend leaving these constrained to the In-App Notification Center.

## 🔇 Muting Specific Threads

Sometimes you leave a quick comment on a viral post, and suddenly your inbox is flooded with hundreds of replies from other users debating the topic. You don't want to disable *all* comment notifications globally, but you desperately want out of that specific thread.

**The Solution:** Mute the thread!
Navigate to the post that is spamming you, click the three-dot menu (Options) in the top right corner of the post, and select **"Mute Notifications for this Post."** You will instantly stop receiving alerts for any future activity on that specific piece of content, while the rest of your notifications remain perfectly intact.

## 📧 Email Notifications

In addition to mobile and web alerts, AnimeType occasionally sends email summaries. These might include weekly recaps of trending anime, announcements about major platform updates, or security alerts (such as a new login from an unrecognized device).

You can manage your email subscriptions in the same **Settings > Notifications** menu. We strictly enforce a no-spam policy. While you can opt out of promotional and recap emails at any time, please note that you cannot opt out of critical security and account recovery emails, as these are necessary to keep your data safe.\n
