# 🔒 Privacy Settings & Account Safety

## Your Safety is Our Priority

At AnimeType, we believe that social media should be a safe, comfortable, and highly customizable experience. You should always have ultimate control over who can see your content, who can interact with you, and what data you share with the public.

Our comprehensive Privacy Settings menu empowers you to tailor your AnimeType experience exactly to your comfort level. You can access these controls at any time by navigating to your Profile, clicking the Gear icon (Settings), and selecting "Privacy & Safety."

## 👁️ Account Visibility (Public vs. Private)

By default, all new AnimeType accounts are set to **Public**. This means that anyone on the internet can view your profile, read your posts, and see your theories. Your content is eligible to appear on the global 'For You' feed and in Search results.

If you prefer a more intimate experience, you can toggle your account to **Private Mode**. 
- **What happens in Private Mode?** Only users who you have explicitly approved as followers can see your posts, your following list, and your profile analytics.
- **Search Visibility**: Your posts will no longer appear in public search results or trending feeds.
- **Follow Requests**: When someone tries to follow you, they will send a "Follow Request," which you must manually approve or deny in your notification center.

## 💬 Controlling Interactions

Even if your account is Public, you can still restrict how people interact with you to prevent spam or harassment.

### Comment Restrictions
You can control who is allowed to leave comments on your posts. In the Privacy Settings, locate the "Who can comment on my posts" section. You have three options:
1. **Everyone**: Any AnimeType user can comment (Default).
2. **Followers Only**: Only people who actively follow you can leave a comment. This is a great way to prevent random trolls from disrupting your discussions.
3. **Nobody**: Completely disables the comment section on all of your posts.

### Mentions and Tagging
Similarly, you can restrict who is allowed to `@mention` you in their posts or comments. If you are receiving unwanted notifications from strangers tagging you, you can change this setting to "Followers Only" or "Nobody."

## 🚫 Blocking and Muting Users

If you encounter a user who is being toxic, posting untagged spoilers, or simply annoying you, you have two powerful tools at your disposal: Muting and Blocking.

### How to Mute a User
Muting is a silent action. When you mute a user, their posts will no longer appear in your feeds, and you will not receive notifications from them. 
- **The catch**: The muted user *does not know* they are muted. They can still see your profile and comment on your posts (though you won't be notified). Muting is best used for users who post annoying content but aren't actively harassing you.

### How to Block a User
Blocking is an absolute action. When you block a user:
- They can no longer see your profile, your posts, or your followers.
- They cannot send you Direct Messages.
- They cannot mention or tag you.
- Any past comments they left on your posts will be hidden from your view.
You can manage your Muted and Blocked user lists directly from the Privacy Settings menu, allowing you to unblock or unmute someone at any time.

## 🟢 Online Status Visibility

AnimeType features a real-time chat system that displays a green dot when you are actively online. If you prefer to browse the app invisibly, you can toggle off "Show Online Status" in the Privacy menu. When disabled, your friends will not be able to see when you are active, though you also will not be able to see their online statuses in return.\n
