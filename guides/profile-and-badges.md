# 👤 Profile Customization, Analytics, & Badges

## Your Digital Identity on AnimeType

Your profile is your home base on AnimeType. It is where you showcase your personality, track your engagement, and display the reputation you have built within the community. We have designed the profile layout to be highly customizable and incredibly data-rich, giving you tools usually reserved for professional content creators.

## 🎨 Customizing Your Profile

To make your profile stand out, navigate to your page and click the "Edit Profile" button. Here you can modify:
- **Display Name**: While your `@username` is unique and permanent, your display name can be changed at any time. Feel free to use emojis, different fonts, or character names!
- **Avatar & Banner**: Upload high-quality images to represent yourself. These images are heavily cached on our global network, ensuring they load instantly for anyone visiting your page.
- **Bio**: You have 160 characters to tell the community who you are. We recommend listing your favorite genres, your current watch-list, or links to your other social media accounts.

## 📊 Deep Dive: Profile Analytics

Unlike other platforms that hide your data from you, AnimeType believes you should have full visibility into how your content is performing. At the top of your profile (visible only to you, unless you choose to share it), you will find the **Analytics Dashboard**.

This dashboard aggregates the performance of all your posts, theories, and comments. 

### Timeframe Filtering
You can filter your analytics by specific time windows using the buttons provided:
- **7 Days**: Great for seeing how your recent posts from the current anime season are performing.
- **30 Days**: A monthly overview of your community growth.
- **365 Days**: A yearly recap of your engagement.
- **All Time**: Your lifetime statistics on AnimeType.

*Note: Because calculating these numbers across thousands of posts is heavy, our system caches your analytics for 1 hour. If you just had a post go viral, check back in an hour to see the massive spike in your dashboard charts!*

## 🏅 The Badge System

As you interact with AnimeType, earn points, and climb the Global Ranking (see our [Ranking Guide](./ranking-and-points.md)), you will inevitably begin unlocking **Badges**.

Badges are beautifully designed visual icons that represent specific achievements. They are a way to instantly signal your expertise and dedication to the community.

### Types of Badges
- **Milestone Badges**: Awarded for reaching specific follower counts, accumulating a certain number of lifetime likes, or hitting a high account level.
- **Specialty Badges**: Awarded for specific actions, such as having a Fan Theory reach over 90% agreement with thousands of votes, or consistently posting high-quality artwork.
- **Event Badges**: Limited-edition badges awarded for participating in community events, voting in seasonal anime polls, or being an early beta tester of AnimeType.

### Displaying Your Badges
You don't have to display every badge you earn. In your profile settings, you can select your top 3 favorite badges to pin directly next to your Display Name. These pinned badges will appear everywhere on the site—next to your comments, on your posts, and in chat threads—allowing you to flex your biggest achievements wherever you go.
