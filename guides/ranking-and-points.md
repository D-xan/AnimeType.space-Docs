# 🏆 Global Ranking & Points System

## What is the Global Ranking System?

AnimeType isn't just a place to post; it's a place to be recognized for your contributions. To reward our most active, insightful, and creative community members, we have implemented a comprehensive **Global Ranking and Points System**. 

Your Global Rank is a reflection of your overall reputation on the platform. As you interact, post high-quality content, and engage in meaningful discussions, you earn points. Accumulating points increases your level, unlocks prestigious profile badges, and pushes you higher up the Global Leaderboard.

## 💯 How the Point System Works

Earning points on AnimeType is designed to be organic. You don't need to game the system; you simply need to be an active, positive participant in the community. Points are awarded automatically in the background for a variety of actions.

Here is a breakdown of how you can earn points:

### 1. Content Creation
- **Creating a Post**: Every time you share a standard post, meme, or piece of artwork, you receive base points for contributing to the feed.
- **Publishing a Fan Theory**: Because Fan Theories require more thought and effort, publishing a theory grants a higher base point reward than a standard post.

### 2. Community Engagement
- **Receiving Likes**: When other users appreciate your content and leave a Like, your score increases. This means that high-quality posts that go viral will significantly boost your ranking.
- **Receiving Comments**: Sparking a discussion is valuable! You earn points when users comment on your posts.
- **Theory Votes (Agree/Interesting)**: If you post a theory and the community votes "Agree" or "Interesting," you are heavily rewarded for providing a compelling hypothesis.

### 3. Active Participation
- **Leaving Comments**: You earn points for actively participating in other users' comment sections (spamming is heavily monitored and will result in point deductions).
- **Daily Logins**: Simply opening the AnimeType app and logging in once a day will grant you a daily streak bonus.

## 📉 Can I Lose Points?

Yes. To maintain a healthy, non-toxic environment, the point system also includes penalties for violating community guidelines.
- If a post or comment you made is reported by multiple users and subsequently removed by our moderation team for toxicity, harassment, or spam, a significant point deduction will be applied to your account.
- Repeated offenses will not only drain your points but may result in a temporary or permanent account ban.

## 🎖️ Levels and Tiers

As your point total grows, your account will automatically level up, placing you into different prestige tiers. While the exact point thresholds are kept secret to prevent farming, the tiers represent your dedication to the AnimeType community:

1. **Rookie**: The starting tier for all new users.
2. **Enthusiast**: Achieved after consistent posting and moderate community interaction.
3. **Veteran**: Reserved for highly active users who regularly receive likes and comments on their content.
4. **Elite**: The highest tier. Elite users are trendsetters, top-tier artists, or master theorists whose content consistently reaches the top of the 'For You' feed.

## 🏅 Why Do Points Matter?

Beyond the fun of watching your numbers go up, points have real value within the AnimeType ecosystem. 
- **Badges**: Reaching specific point milestones automatically unlocks exclusive profile badges that you can display next to your avatar.
- **Visibility**: The algorithm slightly favors content from users with higher reputation scores, meaning reaching the Veteran or Elite tiers helps your posts reach a wider audience.
- **Leaderboard Glory**: The Global Leaderboard showcases the top users on the platform. Securing a spot on the leaderboard is the ultimate bragging right in the AnimeType community.\n
