# Ranking Up Guide

Your progression from **Rookie** to **Anime Legend** depends entirely on your Identity Score.

## The Milestones
Here is the roadmap to the top:
- **Rookie**: Welcome to AnimeType!
- **Academy Student**: You've started interacting.
- **Genin**: You are an established community member.
- **Chunin**: You create consistent content.
- **Jonin**: You are a recognized voice.
- **Kage**: You are an influencer.
- **Legendary Kage**: You have massive reach.
- **Grand Sage**: You are a pillar of the community.
- **Anime Myth**: Your posts set trends.
- **Anime Legend**: The absolute pinnacle of AnimeType.

## Aura Progression
Your Aura upgrades alongside your rank. The coveted **Prism Aura** and **Eclipse Aura** require immense dedication, often achieved by users who consistently publish highly-rated Fan Theories, maintain active Media Dashboards, and foster massive comment sections.

*Pro Tip: Ensure your Anime/Manga Journey is up to date. Marking series as "Completed" provides a steady stream of passive Identity points!*
