# Writing Great Theories

Want to hit the front page with a mind-blowing Fan Theory? Here is how to utilize the Fan Theory Engine effectively.

## 1. Crafting the Perfect Timeline
The **Evidence Timeline** is what separates AnimeType theories from standard forums. 
- Break your argument down into 3-5 key points.
- Provide a **Key Panel Image** for each point. Visual evidence (a screenshot of the anime or a snippet of a manga page) drastically increases your **Agreement Percentage**.

## 2. Navigating the Discussions
When users vote on your theory (Agree, Disagree, Interesting), they will often leave comments. 
The Theory Detail View sorts discussions into **Top Discussions** and **Latest**. Engage with the top comments to defend your points and earn more Identity points from the interactions!

## 3. Collaboration
If you and a friend cooked up a theory together, use the **Invite Collaborator** feature before publishing. Both of you will share the spotlight and the Identity Score boost.
